Brief Biography

I am currently a PhD student with research interests in statistical learning at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, supervised by Prof. Bo An. Before that, I received my B.Sc. degree from College of Science, China Agricultural University, China, in 2021, supervised by Prof. Yitian Xu.
