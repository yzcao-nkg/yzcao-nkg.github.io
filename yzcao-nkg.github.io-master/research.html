Research


My general research interests lie in machine learning, data mining, and artificial intelligence, with a particular emphasis on the following topics:

  • Theory:
    Analysis of the generalization ability of learning algorithms.
    Theoretical understandings of various loss functions.
    Development of unbiased estimators for classification risk in various weakly supervised learning problems.

  • Algorithm:
    Development of effective learning algorithms for various weakly supervised learning problems, including noisy-label learning, semi-supervised learning, positive-unlabeled learning, partial-label learning, multiple-instance learning, and learning from crowds.
    Development of reliable learning algorithms for trustworthy machine learning, including adversarial machine learning, out-of-distribution detection, open-set recognition, and uncertainty-aware deep learning.

  • Application:
    Application of learning algorithms in computer vision and natural language processing.
    Application of weakly supervised learning algorithms in real-world problems with privacy, confidentiality, or security issues.

Feel free to drop me an email if you are interested in collaborating with me on the above research topics.
